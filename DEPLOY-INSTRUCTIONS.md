# 🚀 Deploy to Netlify - Step by Step

## ✅ Your Build is Ready!

The `.output` folder contains everything needed for deployment.

---

## 📋 Method 1: Drag & Drop (EASIEST - 5 minutes!)

### Step 1: Go to Netlify
1. Open your browser
2. Go to: **https://netlify.com**
3. Click **"Sign Up"** (use GitHub, Google, or Email)
4. It's **100% FREE!**

### Step 2: Deploy Your Site
1. Once logged in, you'll see: **"Want to deploy a new site without connecting to Git?"**
2. Click the big **drag-and-drop area**
3. **Drag the entire `.output/public` folder** from your project
   - Location: `C:\Users\HP\OneDrive\Desktop\Birthday-template-lovable\.output\public`
4. Wait 10-30 seconds... ⏳

### Step 3: Get Your URL!
Netlify will give you a URL like:
```
https://cheerful-unicorn-abc123.netlify.app
```

**That's it! Your site is LIVE!** 🎉

### Make it professional:

1. In Netlify dashboard, click **"Site settings"**
2. Click **"Change site name"**
3. Enter: `birthday-template` or your brand name
4. New URL: `https://birthday-template.netlify.app`

## 🔄 How to Update Your Site

### When you make changes:

1. **Edit your code** as needed
2. **Build again:**
   ```bash
   npm run build
   ```
3. **Go to Netlify** dashboard
4. **Drag the `.output/public` folder again**
5. It will update in 10 seconds! ✅
